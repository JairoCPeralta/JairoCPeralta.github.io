About Me

I hold a PhD in Experimental Physics, specializing in atomic spectroscopy and LIBS analysis of liquids and dust using acoustic levitation. My work involved designing experimental setups, developing measurement methods, and analyzing complex datasets.

I enjoy turning ideas into working systems: designing parts in CAD, building prototypes, optimizing experiments, and automating data analysis. Alongside my research, I have teaching experience in physics, mathematics, and programming, strengthening my ability to communicate technical concepts clearly.
